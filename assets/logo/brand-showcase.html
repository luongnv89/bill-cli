BillCLI Brand Identity

Extract structured information from bills and invoices using OCR

Design Rationale

Symbol Meaning

The mark represents a document/invoice with scan lines and an OCR detection circle, embodying the tool's purpose of extracting data from bills.

Typography

Uses Inter — a clean, modern sans-serif that renders sharply at all sizes. The wordmark "BillCLI" combines the product name with the CLI tool indicator.

Color Choices

#0A0A0A (base): Professional, serious tone suitable for financial/billing tools.
#00FF41 (accent): Neon green represents OCR scan detection and digital precision.

Usage Guidelines

Do

  • Use the logo at clear sizes (min 16px for favicon)
  • Maintain padding equal to mark height
  • Use white logo on dark backgrounds
  • Use black logo on light backgrounds

Don't

  • Stretch or distort the logo
  • Rotate or re-angle the mark
  • Add effects (shadows, gradients)
  • Re-color individual elements
